JAMES WEBB SPACE TELESCOPE

A Better View

BY ROB WEBB

Since becoming fully operational in 2022, the James Webb Space Telescope (JWST) has been collecting some of the most breathtaking images of the heavens ever seen. Roughly the size of a tennis court, this $10-billion observatory is the largest and most powerful telescope ever built. The deep field image of a galaxy cluster, called SMACS 0723, is just one example that displays the glory of our God (Psalm 19:1). This is by far one of the most intriguing clusters, showing many “mature” galaxies at distances much farther than secular scientists predicted.
